About Alden Richard


Richard Reyes Faulkerson Jr. (born January 2, 1992), known professionally as Alden Richards, is an American-Filipino entertainer, composer, film producer, director and entrepreneur, founder and CEO of Myriad Corporation, a real estate investor that operates Myriad Real Estate, a game programmer who founded and is CEO of Myriad Esports - one of the first e-sports team management companies in the Philippines. He has featured as an actor in both television and film as well as a recording artist, signed to Universal Records and GMA Pictures.
